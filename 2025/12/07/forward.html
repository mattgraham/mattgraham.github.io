forward
After 11 years we're saying goodbye and stepping into yet again the next chapter.

After 11 incredible years at Heroku and within the Salesforce ecosystem, my family and I have decided it’s time to turn the page and take on a new challenge. I’m filled with deep gratitude for the experiences, growth, and especially the relationships that have shaped both my career and my life. Salesforce has been incredibly good to my family, and I’ll always be thankful for that.

I’ve been fortunate to work alongside some of the most talented, passionate, and brilliant people I’ve ever known. My time here helped me dream bigger, sharpen my skills, and build friendships that I’ll carry with me forever. For more than 20 years I’ve had the privilege of building tools for the developer community, and doing that work with all of you has been a true joy.

To my teammates, partners, mentors, and friends: thank you. Thank you for the trust, collaboration, laughter, problem-solving, and support that made even the toughest projects meaningful. I will genuinely miss working with you day after day.

Next, I’m stepping into a new opportunity as Director of Product at Towbook, a SaaS platform trusted by towing companies around the world. I’ve been familiar with Towbook since its early days and have had the privilege of serving on their advisory board as well as building long-standing friendships with the Towbook team. Joining my good friend Dan Smith on this journey feels both exciting and deeply meaningful, and I’m grateful for the chance to help build the kind of company that transforms how the industry operates. I’m eager to bring everything I’ve learned over the past 11 years to help create something impactful, meaningful, and industry-changing in this new space.

Heres to moving forward!
