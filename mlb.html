MLB Ballpark Tour
So many stadiums, so little time.

There are few things earth like sitting in the stands with the aroma of the game, friends at your side, and hopefully the Detroit Tigers victory. I grew up next to my father and brother in the Tiger Stadium bleachers, and have since set a goal to see as many Major League Ballparks as I am able to. The countdown is to see them all.
